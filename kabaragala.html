Kabaragala

Kabragala is a mountain in the Dolaosbage mountain range. It has the highest summit point in the range.Kabaragala is approximately 1506 meters in height.It has an almost flat terrain at the top similar to Horton Plains and there’s a huge drop similar to the World’s End as well.

The hike takes one along various types of terrain,right through a mountain village and finally up a savanna type grassy slope to the first view point for breath taking views of the valley below. A journey along the ridge from the first view point would take the traveler to the summit. Public Transport to the foothills is available from both Gampola and Nawalapitiya.

The first view point of the mountain could be reached within just 2 hours after starting the climb. Path is clear till the edge of the village and from then onward its savanna with the trees at the top of the hill clearly visible .Road to Craighead, at the start of the trail is in good condition .Expect a degree of leech activity during the rainy season.
