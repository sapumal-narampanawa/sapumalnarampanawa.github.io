Hello, My name is Sapumal, from Sri Lanka

I'm a newbie to the travel photography world and a wanderlust in the land of the smiles "Sri Lanka" to captures the unseen in my own way.

Simply you can call me a traveler to take photographs or a photographer to be in travel. My inspiration to stick to this is the admiration of the others on the captures taken by me and my own happiness of freedom, relaxation and everything that reflected on my own photographs.

Yeah..! I have no such good Dslr camera or lenses, yet my mobile is the best thing to capture the beauty that I am really glad of.

It's my pleasure to share the all beautiful moments and it's your turn to look closer and listen to the untold stories. Each photo has a story to tell, and it’s my job to share that with you. If you’re interested in working together, feel free to contact me.
