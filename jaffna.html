Jaffna

Jaffna is a city located on the northern tip of Sri Lanka. It opened its doors to tourism in 2009. The city has a lot of influence from India, which you can see on the streets, and in their food, which is different from central or south Sri Lanka

Places to visit in Jaffna

  • Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil
  • Nainativu Island
  • Delft (Nedunvitu) Island
  • Jaffna Fort
  • Keerimalai Fresh Water Spring
  • Kankesanthurai Beach Jaffna.
